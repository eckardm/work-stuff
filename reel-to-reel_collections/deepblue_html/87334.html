Eclipse Jazz (University of Michigan) records

The materials in this online repository form part of a larger Eclipse Jazz (University of Michigan) records collection held by the Bentley Historical Library. For a more complete index to the materials, please consult the collection's online finding aid.

For questions or more information, please contact the Bentley Historical Library's Division of Reference and Access Services

Abstract:
University of Michigan student organization founded to promote interest in jazz through concert and lecture series. Audio-tapes of lectures, photographs, posters, announcements of concerts, newsletters, grant files, and scattered correspondence.

History:
Eclipse Jazz was a University of Michigan student organization founded to promote interest in jazz through a series of concerts and lectures.

Please note:

The organization transferred its copyright to the Regents of the University of Michigan.


Access to digitized sound recordings may be limited to the reading room of the Bentley Historical Library, located on the Ann Arbor campus of the University of Michigan.
