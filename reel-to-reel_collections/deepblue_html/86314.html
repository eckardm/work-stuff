Hilary Whittaker papers

The materials in this online repository form part of a larger Hilary Whittaker papers collection held by the Bentley Historical Library. For a more complete index to the materials, please consult the collection's online finding aid.

For questions or more information, please contact the Bentley Historical Library's Division of Reference and Access Services

Abstract:
The Hilary Whittaker papers contains correspondence from her time as Peace Corps volunteer and administrator in India and her time with the Red Cross in Morocco and France.

Biography:
Hilary Whittaker was a volunteer and administrator of the Peace Corps. She also worked with the Red Cross in Morocco and France.
