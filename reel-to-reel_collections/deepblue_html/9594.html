Randy Pratt sound recording

The materials in this online repository form part of a larger Randy Pratt sound recording collection held by the Bentley Historical Library. For a more complete index to the materials, please consult the collection's online finding aid.

For questions or more information, please contact the Bentley Historical Library's Division of Reference and Access Services

History / Biography:
Eye-witness to the attack by gunmen on members of the U.S. House of Representatives on March 1, 1954.

Please note:

Access to digitized sound recordings may be limited to the reading room of the Bentley Historical Library, located on the Ann Arbor campus of the University of Michigan.
